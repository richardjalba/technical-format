The Message

The story of The Tiger and the Strawberry is An Old Zen Story that contains multiple life lessons:

  • In hard times, appreciate the good moments
  • Don't let yourself be distracted when your attention is vital
  • Not making a decision is still a decision
  • Maybe whichever fate you choose won't be pretty, but it's still your choice
  • You choose how to help yourself and what you reach for
